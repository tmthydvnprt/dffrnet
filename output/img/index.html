About

After using a text compare tool at work that is beyond comparison, and not finding anything quite suitable for home use online, I decided to make one! And why not in a little web app? So I mashed this up quickly with big thanks to codemirror & diff-match-patch. Basically, all the heavy lifting is performed by them. I just added a little user interface and the minimap scroller.

Maybe you will find it useful.
Try it. Bookmark it. Share it.
Make suggestions to @dffrnet for improvements.
